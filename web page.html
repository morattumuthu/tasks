HISTORY



Denis Cyplenkov


38-year old Denis Cyplenkov often called “The Ukrainian Hulk” has the heaviest strict curl record in the world and one of the best arm wrestler in the world. Denis is famous for his incredibly large hands and forearms He build up his physique to become the strongest curler in the world, he is also a powerlifter and a strongman




Levan Saginashvili


evan Saginashvili, nicknamed Georgian Hulk, is a Georgian professional arm-wrestler. Winner of the World Armwrestling Championship seven times, the European arm-wrestling championship six times, and the Vendetta Top 8, Saginashvili is the World's number 1 ranking armwrestler at the moment.
